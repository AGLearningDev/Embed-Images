Welcome to AG!

 

 

 

AG Learning & Development. Powered by you. Empowered by us.

Our most valuable asset is our people, and AG’s Learning and Development team are your partners in staying motivated, engaged, and challenged throughout your career with us. We know that joining a new firm can be an exciting and sometimes overwhelming experience. To help you get off on the correct foot, we’ve developed our New Hire Checklist - a complete guide to getting acquainted with AG.

The Checklist details specific action items you need to complete within your first week with us, as well as resources to help you get to know AG and our day-to-day office experience. If you have questions, your team members, mentors and Advisor are here to help!

 

 

 

New Hire Checklist

Check out our New Hire InSite page to complete your checklist!

In conjunction with the Checklist you received during your 1st day orientation, the New Hire Information page on AG InSite functions as a self-service guide to getting settled. This InSite page is meant to be used along with the Checklist and contains all of the supporting resources you need to complete the Checklist steps. As you work through the Checklist, please reference the InSite page for additional details.
